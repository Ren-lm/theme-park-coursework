Passes available at the park


Single Pass

You want to have fun at our park and do not have much time? This pass is for you.

This pass allows you to use any attractions in the park for a whole day. (Please check our opening hours).


Weekend Pass

A whole weekend having fun at our amazing attractions? This is definitely your pass!

This pass allows you to use any attractions in the park for a one weekend (Saturday and Sunday). (Please check our opening hours).


Family Pass

So you have a whole family of fun people? This is the pass for you all!

This pass allows you and up to six people from your family (spouse and children) to use any attractions in the park for one day. (Please check our opening hours).


Annual Pass

You love our park and want to come many times during the year? We've got you covered.

This pass allows you to come to our park as many days as you want during a calendar year. (Please check our opening hours).
